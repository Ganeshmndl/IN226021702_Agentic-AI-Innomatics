Job Title: Data Scientist (Generative AI Focus)
Location: Remote / Pune
Employment Type: Full-time

About the Role:
We are looking for a highly skilled Data Scientist to join our AI engineering team. In this role, you will be responsible for designing, building, and deploying machine learning models and LLM-powered applications. You will work closely with our engineering team to automate internal workflows using the latest Generative AI technologies.

Key Responsibilities:

Design and implement AI pipelines using Large Language Models (LLMs) to solve complex business problems.

Extract, clean, and analyze large datasets to train and fine-tune predictive models.

Monitor, debug, and optimize LLM chains in production to prevent hallucinations and ensure high accuracy.

Collaborate with stakeholders to translate business requirements into technical AI solutions.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

Education: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, or a related field.

Experience: 3+ years of professional experience in Data Science or Machine Learning.

Programming Languages: Proficiency in Python and SQL.

AI & Machine Learning: Strong understanding of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and predictive modeling.

Frameworks & Tools: Hands-on experience with LangChain, Scikit-Learn, and HuggingFace APIs. Experience with tracing and debugging tools like LangSmith is highly desirable.

General Tools: Familiarity with Git, Docker, and Jupyter Notebooks.

What Makes You Stand Out:

Proven track record of deploying LLM applications to production.

Experience with prompt engineering and mitigating AI hallucinations.
